Cookie Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2025  ·  Handover BPO Services  ·  handoverbpo.com

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which cookies Handover BPO Services uses on handoverbpo.com, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences — in full accordance with POPIA.

Section 01 — What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognise your device, remember your preferences, and collect information about how you use the site. Some cookies are essential for the site to work. Others are optional and used for analytics or performance purposes.

Section 02 — Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Necessary for the website to function. Set by the Wix platform for site functionality, session management, and security. Duration: session or up to 12 months.

Analytics Cookies

Allow us to count visits and understand how visitors interact with our site. Information collected is aggregated and anonymous. Set by Wix Analytics for page views, traffic sources, and user behaviour. Duration: up to 13 months.

Marketing & Tracking Cookies

If we run paid advertising campaigns, marketing cookies may be placed on your device. These may be set by Meta Pixel or Google Ads where active. We will only activate marketing cookies where we have obtained your consent via our cookie banner.

Section 03 — Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site are set by third-party services. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies:

  • Wix — wix.com/about/privacy
  • Google Analytics — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Meta (Facebook) — facebook.com/policy.php

Section 04 — Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage cookies via our cookie banner (shown on first visit), or through your browser settings:

  • Chrome — Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox — Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari — Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge — Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Third-party opt-out tools: Google Analytics opt-out  ·  Meta ad preferences

Section 05 — Consent

By continuing to use handoverbpo.com after being presented with our cookie banner, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You may withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your cookie preferences or browser settings. We do not use cookies to collect special personal information or make automated decisions about you.

Section 06 — Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of the page will always reflect the most recent revision.

3. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Cookies help websites remember user preferences, improve website performance, maintain security, analyse website usage, enable functionality, and support user authentication. Cookies do not typically contain information that directly identifies an individual, although information associated with cookies may be linked to other information held by a website operator.

4. Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use web beacons, pixels, local storage, session storage, analytics tags, tracking scripts, log files, and device identifiers. These technologies perform functions similar to cookies and may collect information about interactions with our services.

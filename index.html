The Problem

Vehicles Sit Idle. Nobody Knows Why.

When a vehicle returns to a depot, it needs to go through fuelling, inspection, cleaning, and maintenance before it can go back out. Without a system, nobody knows where any vehicle is in that process, how long it's been sitting, or whether it's actually ready to dispatch. Managers make phone calls, check whiteboards — or simply don't know. Vehicles sit idle longer than they should, and nothing is recorded.

DepotTrack fixes that.

How It Works

QR Code. Scan. Done.

Every vehicle gets a QR code. As it moves through the depot, a staff member scans it at each checkpoint using a phone or tablet. The system records the vehicle location, who scanned it, what time, and the odometer reading.

Live Visibility

The Manager View

A live pipeline showing every vehicle at every checkpoint in real time. See at a glance which vehicles are backed up, which are ready, and which have been sitting too long.

Dwell time at each checkpoint is tracked and colour-coded:

Green — within normal dwell time
Amber — over threshold, needs attention
Red — significantly delayed, action required

Full Feature Set

Beyond the Pipeline

Vehicle Register

A complete record of every vehicle in your fleet — make, model, registration, assigned depot, and current status. The single source of truth for your entire operation.

🔧
Service Interval Tracking

Automated alerts when a vehicle is due or overdue for a service, based on odometer readings captured at each checkpoint scan. Never miss a service again.

📅
Licence Disc Expiry Warnings

Proactive alerts before a vehicle's licence disc expires, so you're never caught operating an unlicensed vehicle. Configurable warning lead times.

🕒
Full Audit Trail

Every scan, every action, every user — logged with a timestamp and user ID. A complete, tamper-evident record of what happened to every vehicle, every time.

📊
8 Report Types

Per-vehicle journey history, operator performance, station summaries, dwell time analysis, fleet utilisation, and more. Export-ready for management review.

📱
Phone & Tablet Ready

Staff scan QR codes on any phone or tablet — no special hardware required. The manager dashboard runs in any browser on any device.
